TEACHING SIMULATION

REGISTRATION FEES: 50 INR per head.

Teaching Simulation is an event where participants will have to show their teaching skills. They will be given a topic and 20 minutes of time to prepare on the same. Participant have to make content of about 10 minutes and present in front of the judges. They will be judge based on their teaching skill, quality of content and style of teaching.

RULES:

  • Participant will be given a topic.
  • Participant will be given 20 minutes time to prepare on given topic.
  • Participant has to prepare minimum of 10 minutes of content and have to teach judges.
  • Judges will judge participant on basis of their teaching skill, quality of content and other aspects.
