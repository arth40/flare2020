SWAAD: THE FOOD BATTLE

REGISTRATION FEES:
1) PDPU students: Rs.500.
2) Non-PDPU students: Rs.1,000.

Make way for the cooking and selling competition! Zaayka is here with the ultimate competition, a competitive flea market, where contestants shall cook and sell their dish. The contestants can cook an entrée or one main dish, but, not deserts. So get your bowls and spatula ready for this vying challenge!

RULES:

  • Contestants may only prepare either appetizers or main course, no desserts.
  • The judgment shall be given in two ways:
    • The judges’ choice shall be made on the basis of taste, decoration, presentation and innovation.
    • And customers’ choice, based on maximum sale, wherein the winner receives a prize.
  • Only 1 dish has to be prepared and sold at the stall. No more than one dish shall be allowed.
  • Only 2 people are allowed per table.
  • Equipment that shall be provided:
    • 1 table and 1 chair, along with a 5A plug point.
    • Soft board, if needed.
  • Only the first 40 entries shall be accepted.
  • The event duration is of 4 hours. The first 30-45 minutes will be allotted for preparation, for the next 2 hours participants can sell their items. Sales will be closed 30 minutes prior to the end of the event.
