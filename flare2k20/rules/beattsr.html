BEAT THE STREET

Street dance is a dance style that has evolved outside dance studios in any available open space such as streets, dance parties, block parties, parks, school yards etc. This includes several dance styles such as breaking, locking, whacking, hip hop, popping and many more. These would be showcased as a cypher and then as a dance battle. The Competition will comprise Two Categories:
(1) 1 VS 1 – All Style Battle
(2) 2 VS 2 – All Style Battle
There will be a preliminary round for both the categories for the selection of TOP 16 (1 VS 1) and TOP 8 (2 VS 2), in which every participant/Duo has to perform for 30 seconds on the random selection of Music.

GENRAL INSTURCTIONS:

  • The Decision of the Judge will be final and there will be no changes made at the request of any participant.
  • Fire, Water and any other disruptive objects are not allowed on the stage.
  • Touching or abusing is not allowed during the battle.
  • The organizing team of BEAT THE STREET reserves the rights to change the rules if they feel there is a need to do so. All the participants are required to adhere to the rules.
  • In case of any delays or interruptions caused (Such as malfunctioning of speakers), the participants will be allowed to continue from the beginning.

1) RULES FOR 1 VS 1 (All Style):

REGISTRATION FEES:100 INR per head.

  • In First Round, all the selected participants will battle against each other. All the participants will have to perform on the random selection of music for 45 seconds. TOP 8 will be selected.
  • In Second Round, Same process will be followed for selection and TOP 4 will be selected.
  • In Semi Finals, all the Participants will have to perform for 1 minutes from which Finalists will be selected.
  • In Finals, There will be two rounds (1 minutes each) from which the STREET CHAMPION will be selected.

2) RULES FOR 2 VS 2 (All Style):

REGISTRATION FEES: 200 INR per team.

RULES:

  • In First Round, all the selected duos will battle against each other. In this round all the duos will have to perform on the random selection of music for 1 minute. Top 4 will be selected.
  • In Second Round, Same process will be followed for selection and Finalist will be selected.
  • In Finals, there will be two rounds (1 minutes each) from which the STREET CHAMPION DUO will be selected.
