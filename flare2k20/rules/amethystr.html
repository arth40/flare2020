AMETHYST

REGISTRATION FEES: 100 INR per head.

Amethyst is a national level model hunt hosted by PANACHE, wherein students from across the country are welcome to participate in and win a pageant. It is essential for participants to presently be students of a university. It comprises three rounds, two during the auditions and one on the main day. The first qualification round shall be based off the contestant’s walk, poise, etc. The second round shall be conducted on the day after that of the first round where the participants will be placed in front of cameras for a photography round before qualifying for the main day’s events. The shortlisted participants will then go through a weeklong grooming session by a team of experts until the main day which shall see the 20 models strut as they run through an Indian and a Western round.

RULES FOR AUDITIONS:

  • Only individual participation is allowed.
  • Incorrect information will result in disqualification whether discovered before, during or after participation.
  • The participant must carry their college ID card and payment receipts if received.
  • On the spot, registrations start at 9 a.m. while confirmation of online registration will start at 10 am.
  • All the participants are requested to reach the premises by 11:45 a.m. latest. The registration desk closes at 11:45 a.m. No participant will be entertained after that
  • Auditions start at 12 p.m. and shall be carried out in the PDPU Auditorium. Auditions will be on 4th April’20.
  • Female participants are required to wear heels and male participants should wear closed shoes for the auditions.
  • No obscene language will be tolerated.
  • The use of materials like candles, matches, cigarettes and alcohol or any other hazardous materials within the premises is prohibited.
  • The auditions for AMETHYST 6.0 will comprise two rounds; the first qualification round shall be based on the contestant's walk, poise, stage presence, etc. and the second round shall/will be conducted on the day after the first round. Participants will be placed in front of cameras for the photography round before qualifying for the main day’s event.
  • A total of 40 participants, 20 boys, and 20 girls will qualify on the first day for the photography round based on their ability to photograph well, 20 participants shall be shortlisted for the main day, 10 boys and 10 girls.
  • The decision of the judges will be final in all cases.
  • Potential winners will be notified by email and telephone.
  • Travel and stay expenses will have to be borne by the participants.
