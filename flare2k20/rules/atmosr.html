AT-MOSH-SPHERE 8.0

REGISTRATION FEES: 1000 INR per team.

At-Mosh-Sphere is a nationwide battle of bands hosted by Offbeat – The Music Club of PDPU, which offers an opportunity to novice and accomplished bands to showcase their way around different genres of music. Members of a headlining band will judge the performances and declare the winner. The Bands can perform either a composition of their own or a cover to win exciting prizes.

COMMON RULE FOR AUDITIONS AND MAIN EVENT

  • The tracks should primarily consist of Hindi and/or English lyric; a brief deviation to any foreign language will be excused.
  • Purely instrumental performances are not allowed.
  • The team should consist of 3-8 members; amongst which, aspects mentioned below are mandatory.
  • 1 vocalist, 2 different instruments (different guitars will be counted individually) and 1 percussion instrument
  • At least one of the members should be enrolled in any of the government recognized university/college
  • Age of any member of the team should not exceed 26 years
  • Exceeding the time limit will lead to a penalty. The time limit for Auditions and Finale is mentioned later on in the rulebook.
  • The declared line-up for the event will ONLY be modified at the discretion of organizers.
  • The entry of any band will only be considered after the payment of the registration fees has been received.
  • Personal ID cards will be checked at the time event. Moreover, contestants should be able to provide the IDs whenever asked.
  • Contestants are required to report to their appointed crew members 10 minutes before their performance.
  • Obscenity (at the discretion of the judges) of any kind is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification. In case of any unruly behavior/misbehavior with the organizing team, the defaulter band is liable to disqualification straight away.
  • In case of any dispute, the decision of the judges and/or organizers will be final and non-negotiable
  • The bands are required to register online through the link provided On Flare website and off beat’s Instagram page.
  • The event date and other essential data will be provided on the Flare website and Instagram page of Offbeat as well as it will be e-mailed to the registered IDs.
  • Audition videos shouldn’t be any YouTube videos and the audio video clarity of the video submitted should be HD. Organizers are not responsible if the participant team gets rejected based on their audio video quality.

VIDEO ENTRIES

  • Bands are required to share links of their own Google Drive to offbeat@pdpu.ac.in. They must register themselves through the link shared on the Flare’s website and Instagram page of Offbeat.
  • The video should be authentic and should cover a live performance in which all Participating members are involved. If defied, the team will be disqualified.
  • The duration of your video entry must be 2-3 minutes.
  • The selected entries will be declared on The Flare website, Offbeat Instagram page, as well as an e-mail will be sent to notify the members personally.
  • The filename of the video should be of the format-AMS8 [registration no.][College Name][Band Name] E.g.-AMS812345PDPURadiohead
  • Not abiding this format will lead to the disqualification of the band.

MAIN EVENT (Grand Finale):

The rules for the main event are the same as those for the auditions, except for the following changes:

  • Bands have to perform at least 1 cover song of their choice along with at least 1original composition.
  • Covers once performed in auditions cannot be performed in the finale.
  • The time limit in the main event for participants is 12 minutes for performance and 3 minutes for sound check.
  • A drum kit (inclusive of 1 bass drum, 2 rack toms, 1-floor tom,1 hi-hat, 2 cymbals, and 1 snare drum), 3 microphones, 6 input lines, and a sound system will be provided by the organizers. If any band requires more than 6 input lines then they are supposed to inform the organizers a week before the event.
  • No more than 2 people are allowed to accompany the bands to the console.

HOSPITALITY:

  • Paid accommodation shall be provided to the bands if applied in advance.
  • All bands wishing to avail accommodation must contact the Hospitality In-charge well in advance.

JUDGING CRITERIA

  • Quality of vocals.
  • Instrumental prowess tightness
  • Synchronization
  • As a band impact on the audience.
