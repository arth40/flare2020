NETFLIX AND QUIZ

REGISTRATION FEES: 50 INR per head.

RULES:

  • There will be a preliminary written round after which, 6-8 teams will be selected for the finals.
  • You can participate individually or as a team of two.
  • The selected teams will compete with each other over the course of 2 – 3 rounds which may comprise of bounce – pounce, written & audio – video questions.
  • The quizmaster’s verdict is paramount.
  • Usage of phones is strictly prohibited.
