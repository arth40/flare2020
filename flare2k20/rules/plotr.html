PLOT-O-RACTER

REGISTRATION FEES: 50 INR per head.

There are many possible endings to the same story, and everyone views it differently on an individual level. Alternate plot gives the participants independence to decide for what could have been a better ending for a particular story. Alternate plot aims to make the contestants explore for how their brains work when it comes to creating imaginary situations.

RULES:

  • The alternate plot must be original. Any plagiarism of any kind shall subject your entry to instant disqualification.
  • Alternate plot length should preferably not exceed 300 words.
  • The tense of the alternate plot needs to be maintained such that it can replace the chosen plot without damaging the flow of the chapter.
  • The source of the original plot must be mentioned.
