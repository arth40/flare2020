ANGUISH

Anguish is the event for every enthusiastic gamer who like to play games that not just provide a source of recreation but also help enhance memory and concentration.

GENERAL RULES & REGULATIONS:

  • Players are expected to bring their own gaming accessories (especially headset and mouse)
  • A player can join only one team.
  • Any kind of cheats like wall hack and aim lock is strictly restricted. If any player is caught cheating, his team will be dismissed and another team shall be declared as the winner.
  • Default skins must be used.
  • Only one game entry will be free for Club’s ex core committee members.
