N’ZEST

PDPU brings to you the national solo and duet dance competition for people whose passion is dance. This competition has witnessed marvelous performances by numerous participants before, and continues to showcase truly promising participants with the most brilliant ability every year.

AUDITIONS GUIDELINES(Both Solo & Duet):

  • The Auditions shall be done through submission of videos by the participants tokeval.mch17@sot.pdpu.ac.in.
  • The maximum length of the video Audition clip shall be 2 minutes.
  • The last date to submit the Audition clip is 6th April 2020.
  • The shortlisted participants will be communicated the results by the N'Zest organizing committee a few days prior to the final performance date.

GENERAL GUIDELINES:

  • • Every participant is required to bring their respective college identity cards as well as one photocopy of the same.
  • The song must be submitted on a pen drive and ONLY IN THE MP3 format at least 2 hours before the finals.
  • While submitting the song/soundtrack, it’s the responsibility of the participant to make sure that the soundtrack is saved by the correct name of the participant and his/her college and in the correct slot number. It’s also the responsibility of the participant to verify whether the soundtrack plays without any error once copied onto the system.
  • In case the props (only tables and chairs) are needed the organizers must be informed at least 7 days before the dance.
  • Judges’ result will be final and binding.
  • Any sort of nuisance backstage will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Vulgarity on the stage is strictly forbidden. On doing so; may lead to direct disqualification.
  • Fire or any colorful materials like Gulal etc. are NOT allowed on stage.
  • Participants have to take care of their own props. The Organizing Committee won’t be responsible for any mishap, or the missing of the props.

SOLO EVENT : JHANKAAR (Indian Dance – Classical/Folk) & MOULIN ROUGE (Western Dance)

REGISTRATION FEES: 100 INR per head.

RULES:

  • Preliminary rounds will be subject to the number of entries.
  • Time limit for the final round will be Minimum 2 minutes & Maximum 4 minutes.
  • Props are allowed.

DUET EVENT : TWO-TO-TANGO

REGISTRATION FEES: 200 INR per team

RULES:

  • The dancing partners can be of opposite or same gender.
  • Time limit for the final round will be a minimum of 2 minutes & maximum 5 minutes.
  • Props are allowed.
  • If a person is used as a prop, he/she is not allowed to dance.
