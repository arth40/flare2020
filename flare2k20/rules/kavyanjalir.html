KAVYANJALI

REGISTRATION FEES: 100 INR per head.

Breathe in experience, breath out poetry. The flagship Poetry Recitation Competition by Jharokha- the literature club of PDPU, marks the burst of talent and ambition of young artists. The evening of overture, exposition, and sentiments holds the fort for performers of literature.

Kavyanjali has two categories:

  • Self-composed
  • Adapted

Under these categories, you may recite your poem in either of the four languages:

  • English
  • Hindi
  • Gujarati
  • Urdu

RULES:

  • Participants performing in other languages are welcome, however they will not be judged.
  • If you wish to perform more than one poem, you must register for each poem individually.
  • The time limit for each performance is 5 minutes.
  • Participants cannot register more than one poem in the same sub-category.

NOTE:

All registered participants are requested to bring three printed copies of their poems and reach the venue 30 minutes prior to the session.
