EMOTIONS

Acting is not being emotional, but being able to express emotions! Emotions, is a national level theatre competition. It provides theatre enthusiasts a platform to exhibit their talent through varied forms of theatre ranging from stage plays, to mime, to monologues and even stand-up comedy. These power packed performances assure to leave you stunned with awe!

AUDITION GUIDELINES:

  • For Auditions a synopsis and video entry needs to be submitted for stage play, mono act, mime and stand ups.
  • Auditions will be conducted on the basis of full script synopsis that need to be mailed to samikkkhoja18@gmail.com latest by 4th April, 2020.
  • Time duration for mono acts would be 2-3 minutes. Time duration for stand up is 3-4 minutes long. Time duration for mime is 3-4 minutes. Time duration for Stage Play is 10-15 minutes.
  • The video must be taken in a single shot. Any type of editing or effects will lead to disqualification of the participant/team.
  • The video will be taken into consideration after fees are paid.
  • Video must be in mp4 format.
  • The last date of accepting video auditions and synopsis is 4th April, 2020. No further extension will be accepted.

GENERAL RULES:

  • All members of a team are to carry their ID cards. Participants are to maintain the decorum and sanctity of the University. The University rules would be applicable to all whenever on the premises.
  • The content should not be demeaning to any particular caste, creed, religion or faith. Doing so will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Judges’ decisions will be final and binding.
  • In case of a tie, the prize money will be divided between the winning teams.
  • Participants should reach the venue one hour prior to the event.
  • Auditions will be conducted on the basis of Full Script Synopsis that need to be mailed to samikkkhoja18@gmail.com latest by 4th April, 2020.

STAND-UP:

REGISTRATION FEES: 100 INR per head.

RULES:

  • On the basis of performance in auditions (tentative), few selected participants will perform in the finale.
  • Participants will have to reach the venue on time.
  • Any obscenity and vulgarity will not be allowed. This would lead to immediate disqualification. Any attempt to hurt sentiments of any particular community will be taken as a serious offence.
  • Any kind of music (if required) has to be in mp3 format on a Pen drive.

STAGE PLAY:

REGISTRATION FEES: 1200 INR per team.

RULES:

  • Play could be in English, Hindi or Bi-lingual.
  • Team size: 15 members (including backstage crew and technical assistants). One team member must be present with the light and one with the sound man during the performance.
  • A week before the event, the synapse of the play should be mailed to the organizers.
  • Time limit: empty to empty stage – 60 minutes. Time limit should be strictly followed.
  • Limited chairs and tables would be provided. Any other props have to be arranged by participants themselves. Participants have to inform the organizers in advance about all the logistics required like the number of tables and chairs etc.
  • All the teams must bring their music (if any) in mp3 format in Pen Drive.
  • Any obscenity and vulgarity is not allowed. This would lead to immediate disqualification. Any attempt to hurt sentiments of any particular community will be taken as a serious offence.
  • Do not use any type of liquid, fire or color on the stage.

RULES:
