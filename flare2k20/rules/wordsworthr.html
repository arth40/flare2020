WORDSWORTH

REGISTRATION FEES: 50 INR per head.

A game testing one's way with creating words out of scrambled letters; this is a competition with three rounds of an addictive game that ends either in happy satisfaction or in utter frustration.

RULES:

  • The teams will be given a specific time limit according to the level of the rounds.
  • No usage of any smart-device is allowed.
  • Participants must abide by the instructions given by the host.
  • Participation is accepted in pairs or as individuals.
