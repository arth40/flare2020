ART BATTLE

REGISTRATION FEES: 50 INR per Head.

It is a series of art competitions where the participant will have to pass through three stages of the Fine Arts activities. Each stage in itself is an elimination round and requires the participants to use their creativity to the maximum. The participant which will successfully clear all the three stages will be declared as the winner.

RULES:

  • A team must consist of 2 people.
  • There will be three rounds in this competition.
  • Time given to participants will be 3 hours.
  • Judging criteria of this competition is based on creativity, uniqueness, finishing, overall impact and other round specific criteria.
  • All the relevant material like acrylic colors, pencils, rubbers, sharpeners etc. will be provided to the participants free of cost which needs to be returned at the end of the event.
  • Teams will have to participate in a series of fine arts related tasks. Participation in every task is compulsory.
  • The material for the next round will be provided only after the completion of the previous round.
  • Other tools are not allowed to be used except the kit provided by the organizers.
  • Use of unfair means, explicit content or references is prohibited and shall lead to immediate disqualification.
  • There are limited entries and they are on first come first serve basis. Only first 30 entries will be taken.
  • Participants are advised to arrive 30 minutes prior to the venue of the competition.
  • The decision of the judge shall be final and binding.
