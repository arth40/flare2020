WRITER'S DESK

REGISTRATION FEES: 50 INR per head.

Imagination is a mighty power humans possess. While reading a story, one gets completely immersed into it due to Imagination. Writing prompt is a platform where one can show how flexible their imaginative powers are and have a medium to express their creativity.

  • In case of any discrepancies, the judges' decision is final.
  • Use of any kind of electronic device is strictly prohibited.
