PUN - TOON

REGISTRATION FEES: 50 INR per head.

RULES:

  • Only individual participation is permitted.
  • This competition consists of only one round.
  • Judging criteria pertains to creativity, incorporation of theme, finishing, uniqueness, color scheme and overall impact of the sketch/doodle.
  • Materials provided will include, A/3 sheet, oil pastels, pencil, eraser, sharpener, black sketch pen. All the material provided are free of cost and should be returned to the club members at the end of the event. Participants may only carry their own charcoals if they want.
  • Time limit for the participants would be 2 hours.
  • Participants need to make a pun intended sketch on a given theme (to be told on the spot).
  • Other tools are not allowed to be used except the kit provided by the organizers.
  • No pictures or actual samples of the same are allowed to be carried by the participants to the event. Use of unfair means, explicit content or references is prohibited and shall lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Sheets will be provided only once. Participants are requested not to spoil them.
  • There are limited entries & they are based on first come first serve, so even if you register online there is a possibility that you may not able to participate if entries are full.
  • Participants are advised to come 20 minutes prior to venue of the competition.
  • The decision of the judges shall be final binding.
