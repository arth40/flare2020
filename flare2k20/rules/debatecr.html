DEBATE CHAMPIONSHIP

REGISTRATION FEES: 150 INR per head.

RULES:

  • Teams will be comprised of 2 members.
  • Each debate shall have 2 teams.
  • The competition format is head-to-head knockout; defeat means elimination.
  • A set of topics for each round will be preset by the organizers.
  • Once the topic is determined, a coin toss will determine for/against, with the former speaking first.
  • Each team will be given 30 minutes to prepare for a debate.
  • Maximum speaking time is 10 minutes, to be divided amongst the team at their discretion.
  • Questions (Points of Information) are permitted at the discretion of the speaker.
  • Judges will be selected by organizer and a guarantee of unbiased judgment is given by the same.
  • All decisions of the judges are binding and final.
  • Languages permitted: English and Hindi.
