AAHVAN- STREET PLAY COMPETITION

REGISTRATION FEES:500 INR per team.

Aahvan is the national level street play competition, wherein the street play teams come along with their phenomenal street plays aiming to compete and raise their voice against social issues. This is an ideal platform for various such teams to showcase this art form with an aim to raise awareness.

RULES:

  • Team size: Minimum-10 Members; Maximum-21 members (including percussionist)
  • Language used can be Hindi or English or bilingual.
  • Script should be self-written/original. If found guilty, it would result in disqualification.
  • Recorded music and electronic instruments are not allowed. Only live music is allowed.
  • Any musical instrument has to be arranged by participants themselves.
  • Time limit: 20 minutes (Exceeding time limit will result in Negative Marking).
  • Participating teams must report at least 1 hour prior to the event.
  • Decision of judges will be final and binding. In case of a tie, prize money will be shared by the winning teams.
  • Any obscenity and vulgarity will not be allowed. This would lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Non-adherence to the rules will result in disqualification.
