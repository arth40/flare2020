COSPLAY CON

REGISTRATION FEES: 100 INR per head.

The ultimate PDPU cosplay convention is here! Cosplayers from across the nation and university come together for an epic showdown of the most creative design and invented costumes from the Anime and the Pop-Culture world. The outfits can be elaborate and detailed, or simple and minimalistic. Going beyond the usual meet and greet realms of the convention, this event has games, quizzes and many such surprise elements for you to enjoy!

RULES:

  • Cosplayers are supposed to wear their own costumes and/or any other requirements prior to coming for the event.
  • University rules will be applicable to anyone who's within the university premises.
  • Not safe for work costumes are strictly prohibited.
  • No inappropriate physical contacts are allowed during the convention and the photo session. Strict actions will be taken against those who inappropriately behave with the cosplayers.
  • Participants are requested to show up at the venue at least 30 minutes prior to the event.
  • The best costume will be judged on the basis of: Best concept, Best Handwork, Originality and Looks.
  • In case of a tie, prize money will be split between the winners.
  • In case of any discrepancy, the decision of the organizing committee shall be final and binding.
  • The organizers are not responsible for any loss or physical injury during the progression of either of these events.
  • The upper age limit to participate in this event is 23.
