MELODIA’20

A national-level music competition organized by Offbeat - The Music Club PDPU, who believe music is not a confined art form but is limitless! Participants can perform in the following categories – Indian solo, western solo, duet, band, instrumental, rap, and beat boxing to win exciting prizes.

GENERAL RULES AND REGULATIONS:

  • Participants are expected to reach the event venue 30 minutes prior to the event.
  • Personal ID cards will be checked at the time of registration. Moreover, contestants should be able to provide the IDs whenever asked.
  • Participants must not exceed the given time limits. Marks will be deducted for doing so.
  • Declared line-up for the event will ONLY be modified at the discretion of organizers.
  • A drum kit (inclusive of 1 bass drum, 2 rack toms, 1-floor tom, 1 hi-hat, 2 cymbals, and 1 snare drum) will be provided by the organizers. Other instruments are supposed to be brought by the participants themselves. For any additional requests, participants are to contact the crew in advance.
  • 5 input lines and 3 microphones will be provided. If any performer requires more than 5 input lines and/or 3 microphones they are required to inform the organizers a week prior to the event.
  • Karaoke will not be provided, it must be brought by the participant and they shall submit it to the organizers before the event.Backing tracks should be in MP3 format and to be brought in a pendrive or to be sent by e-mail to the organizers on offbeat@pdpu.ac.in. Tracks will not be accepted once the event starts if the participant misses the deadline they will have to perform without the track.
  • Obscenity (at the discretion of the judges) of any kind is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification. In case of any unruly behavior/misbehavior with the organizing team, or judges the defaulter band/performer is liable to disqualification straightaway.
  • The decision of the judges will be deemed final and no further requests will be entertained in the matter.

RULES FOR INDIAN SOLO:

REGISTRATION FEES: 100 INR per head.:

Auditions:

  • No backing tracks are allowed. However, Participants may play an instrument themselves.
  • The time limit is 3 minutes.
  • Songs in official Indian languages except English will be considered as a part of this category.

Finale:

  • Backing tracks are allowed. However, participants may play an instrument themselves or use one accompanist or use a karaoke track.
  • Participants will have a performance time of 4 - 5 minutes.
  • Songs in Indian languages except for English will be considered as a part of this category.

RULES FOR WESTERN SOLO:

REGISTRATION FEES: 100 INR per head.

Auditions:

  • No backing tracks are allowed. However, participants may play an instrument by themselves.
  • The time limit is 3 minutes.
  • Songs in languages other than Indian languages will be considered as a part of this category. Languages apart from English are also allowed.

Finale:

  • Backing tracks are allowed. However, Participants may play an instrument themselves or use one accompanist or use a karaoke track.
  • Participants will have a performance time of 4 - 5 minutes
  • Songs in languages other than Indian languages will be considered as a part of this category. Languages apart from English are also allowed.

RULES FOR DUET:

REGISTRATION FEES: 200 INR per team.

Auditions:

  • No backing tracks are allowed.
  • Since it is a duet, only two people can participate at a time; and one or both of them can use an instrument and both of them have to sing.
  • Songs in Hindi and English (as well as Mash-ups) are allowed.
  • The time limit for the auditions is 4 minutes.

Finale:

  • Vocals are not allowed.
  • Backtracks are not allowed
  • Participants will have a performance time of 6-7 minutes.
  • One original composition of at least 2 minutes is compulsory.

RULES FOR RAP:

REGISTRATION FEES: 100 INR per head.

Auditions:

  • The language of rap must be only English and/or Hindi.
  • The participants may use any karaoke track.
  • The time limit will be of 90 seconds.
  • Use of swears, sexual slangs, any sort of racist, sexist or offensive slurs will lead to immediate disqualification along with the penalty of Rs. 500.

Finale:

  • Participants will have a performance time of 90-120 seconds.
  • Performance performed in the audition cannot be performed in finale.
  • The participants may use any karaoke track.
  • The language of rap must be only English and/or Hindi.
  • Use of swears, sexual slangs, any sort of racist, sexist or offensive slurs will lead to immediate disqualification along with the penalty of Rs. 500.

RULES FOR BEATBOXING:

REGISTRATION FEES: 100 INR per head.

Auditions:

  • The time limit will be of 90 seconds.
  • No instruments or backtracks are allowed.

Finale:

  • Participants will have a performance time of 90-120 seconds.
  • Performance performed in audition cannot be performed in finale.
  • No instruments or backtracks are allowed.

RULES FOR BAND:

REGISTRATION FEES: 300 INR per team.

Auditions:

  • There should be at least one vocalist in a group/band.
  • The band can perform covers as well as original compositions. However, for the finale, the band has to perform an original composition. OC accompanied by a cover is allowed.
  • The time limit will be 5 minutes.
  • Participants have to bring their instruments. Only the drum-kit will be provided by the organizers.

Finale:

  • Performing an original composition is mandatory. OC may or may not be accompanied by a cover.
  • The time limit will be 10 minutes.
