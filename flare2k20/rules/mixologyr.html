MIXOLOGY

REGISTRATION FEES: 100 INR per head.

Who said making a mocktail is boring? Zaayka presents a mind-stirring competition where in contestants mix two drinks and make a delicious mocktail! Contestants can get as creative as they want and create a variety of mocktails, slushes, crushes, masala drinks, and what not!

RULES:

  • Ice cubes will be provided to the contestants on the spot.
  • Judgment will be on the basis of creativity and taste.
  • Equipment provided will be 1 table and 1 chair.
  • Only the first 30 entries will be accepted.
